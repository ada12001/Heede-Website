FEATURED — 05 PROJECTS DALLAS · CHARLOTTE · BELTON · CLEVELAND · TOPEKA
01

2811 Maple & 23 Springs

LOCATION
Dallas, TX
CONTRACTOR
Balfour Beatty Construction & DPR Construction
SCOPE
2 projects · 2 contractors · all Team Heede

Crescent is developing The Residences at 2811 Maple, a 31-story, 177-unit luxury multifamily high-rise in Dallas' Uptown submarket. 23Springs rises 26 stories at Cedar Springs and Maple Avenue, presenting a new kind of office experience for Dallas' workforce.

02

Legacy Union 6Hundred

LOCATION
Charlotte, NC
CONTRACTOR
Shelco / Gilbane
SCOPE
24 stories · 415,000 SF Class A office

An iconic 24-story, 415,000-square-foot Class A office building at Charlotte's new center city — Brooklyn Village Avenue and Tryon Street — with ground-floor retail, podium parking, conference, co-working and fitness spaces, terraces, and architecture that contrasts the traditional 33-story Bank of America Tower.

03

Bell County Jail Expansion

LOCATION
Belton, TX
CONTRACTOR
Sedalco Construction Services
CRANE
21 LC 750 · 82,670 lb max, six-part line

A jail expansion adding hundreds of beds and resources. The project includes 78 prefabricated cell blocks weighing 33,745 to 55,945 lbs — Heede's 21 LC 750, with 82,670 lb maximum capacity in six-part line, provided the muscle.

04

City Club Apartments

LOCATION
Cleveland, OH
CONTRACTOR
Cleveland Construction
SCOPE
23 stories · 304 residences · mixed-use

A 23-story post-tensioned concrete mixed-use residential high-rise in downtown Cleveland with 304 apartments and penthouses, resort-class amenities, retail, a restaurant / speakeasy, cafe, dog daycare, and a rooftop park, pool, lounge and garden.

05

Docking State Office Building

LOCATION
Topeka, KS
CONTRACTOR
Hutton
CRANE
21 LC 550 · demo + rebuild

A 14-story, 65-year-old building near the Kansas statehouse. Heede's 21 LC 550 is used to demolish the existing building and build a new one — salvaging marble, granite, and statues along the way.
